Chronicle

Where documents are decided, not just edited.

Product Vision & Feature Specification

Version 0.1 ‚Äî Internal Working Document

1. The Problem

  -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  Every organisation is making decisions in documents every day. The decision ‚Äî and the reasoning behind it ‚Äî disappears the moment the comment is resolved. Six months later, nobody can answer why.
  -----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Modern knowledge tools are optimised for writing and reading. None of them treat deliberation as a first-class citizen. Confluence gives you a wiki. Notion gives you a database. Google Docs gives you a collaborative word processor. All of them have the same fundamental flaw:

+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Comments vanish                                                                                | No real approval                                                                                  | Decisions scatter                                                                                |
|                                                                                                |                                                                                                   |                                                                                                  |
| When a comment is resolved it disappears. The reasoning behind every edit is permanently lost. | Page approvals are cosmetic. Nothing actually blocks publication until the right people sign off. | The real decision happens in Slack, email, or a meeting. The document just shows the conclusion. |
+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

This is expensive. Teams re-litigate settled decisions. New hires have no way to understand why the architecture looks the way it does. Auditors cannot reconstruct who approved what and when. Regulated industries work around this with paper trails and email chains.

Chronicle solves this by making the deliberation permanent, structured, and queryable ‚Äî without changing how people write.

2. What Chronicle Is

Chronicle is an open-source, self-hostable document collaboration platform built on a Git backend. It combines the editing experience of a modern word processor with the version control rigour of a software repository ‚Äî and adds a layer that no competitor has: a permanent, structured record of every decision made in or around a document.

+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| For engineering teams                                                                                                                                                                                                      | For everyone else                                                                                                                                                                                                       |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                            |                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| Replace Confluence. Write RFCs, architecture decisions, runbooks, and policies in a tool that actually tracks who proposed what, who objected, and what was finally agreed. Branch-based editing means no more page locks. | Replace SharePoint, Word round-trips, and email chains. WYSIWYG editing with approval workflows that cannot be bypassed. Every annotation, every tracked change, every sign-off ‚Äî permanently attached to the document. |
+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

Core Philosophy

-   The document is the source of truth ‚Äî not the meeting, not the Slack thread, not the email

-   Deliberation is as important as the conclusion ‚Äî both must be preserved

-   Approval must have teeth ‚Äî you cannot merge until the right people sign off

-   Open source by default ‚Äî self-hostable, no vendor lock-in, no per-seat pricing at scale

-   Git underneath, invisible on top ‚Äî all the power of version control with none of the vocabulary

3. Who Uses Chronicle

Chronicle has two distinct but equally important user populations. The primary beachhead is engineering and technical teams ‚Äî the same people who currently use Confluence. The second wave is regulated and professional industries where document governance is a compliance requirement, not a preference.

Primary: Engineering & Technical Orgs

+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Pain they feel today                                                                                                                                                                                   | Why they switch                                                                                                                                                                                          |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                        |                                                                                                                                                                                                          |
| Confluence is slow, expensive ($10‚Äì15/user/month), and being force-migrated to Cloud. The editor is widely mocked. Comments on ADRs and RFCs disappear. Merge decisions live in Slack, not in the doc. | Chronicle is free to self-host. The decision log is a genuine differentiator for engineering orgs that value ADRs. Branch-based editing solves the page-lock problem. Import from Confluence on day one. |

Secondary: Regulated Professional Services

+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Law firms                                                                                                                                                         | Engineering consultancies                                                                                            | Financial services                                                                                                                       |
|                                                                                                                                                                   |                                                                                                                      |                                                                                                                                          |

4.3 Deliberation & Discussion

+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Block-anchored Threads ‚úì Core                                                                                                                                                                      |

+----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Threaded Replies ‚úì Core                                                                                                                                 |

+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Annotation Types ‚úì Core                                                                                                     |
|                                                                                                                             |
| Classify annotations by type: General, Legal, Commercial, Technical, Security, Query, Editorial. Filter and search by type. |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Resolve with Record ‚úì Core                                                                                                                                                      |
|                                                                                                                                                                                 |

+---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Decision Log ‚úì Core                                                                                                                                                                                       |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                           |

+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Suggestion Mode ‚úì Core                                                                                                                                                 |
|                                                                                                                                                                        |
| Users without edit access can submit suggestions ‚Äî proposed changes that appear as tracked changes for the author to accept or reject. No direct edit access required. |

7. Open Source Strategy

Chronicle is open-core. The core platform is MIT licensed and fully functional without a commercial license. Revenue comes from hosted Chronicle Cloud and from enterprise features.

+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Chronicle OSS (MIT)                                                                                                                                                                       | Chronicle Cloud / Enterprise                                                                                                                                                 |
|                                                                                                                                                                                           |                                                                                                                                                                              |
| Full WYSIWYG editor. Version control and branching. Discussion threads. Decision log. Approval chains. Spaces and permissions. REST API. Self-host via Docker or Kubernetes. Always free. | Managed hosting with SLA. SSO/SAML/SCIM. Advanced audit logs. Dedicated support. Data residency options. Confluence migration service. DocuSign integration. Volume pricing. |
+-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

  ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  The OSS strategy is intentional: it removes the single biggest barrier to adoption for IT-gated organisations. An engineer can self-host Chronicle for their team without a procurement process. Enterprise features come when the organisation scales.

Chronicle‚Äôs permission model is designed for organisations that have both internal collaborators and external stakeholders ‚Äî clients, auditors, contractors, regulators ‚Äî who need controlled access to documents without becoming full workspace members. It is the most common failure point in enterprise document tools, and one of the primary reasons organisations stay on SharePoint or email despite hating both.

+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Internal members                                                                                                                                                                                                 | External guests                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
|                                                                                                                                                                                                                  |                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              |
| Full workspace members authenticated via email/SSO. Assigned to groups and spaces. Subject to role-based permissions. Appear in approval chains and @mentions. Governed by SCIM provisioning and deprovisioning. | Outside the organisation. Access limited to specific documents or spaces they are explicitly invited to. Cannot see anything beyond their grant. No access to internal discussion unless explicitly shared. Tracked separately in audit logs. Time-limited access supported. |
+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

8.3 Internal vs External Discussion Visibility

This is the most nuanced part of the permission model ‚Äî and the most important for professional services use cases. Internal teams need to deliberate candidly about a document before sharing it externally. That internal deliberation must never be visible to external parties.

8.5 The Client Portal Use Case

Chronicle‚Äôs external sharing model enables a high-value use case that no current tool handles well: the client portal. A law firm, consultancy, or financial adviser can use Chronicle as a live collaboration layer with their clients, without exposing their internal deliberation.

+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+
| What the law firm sees                                                                                                                                                | What the client sees                                                                                                                                                                                               |
|                                                                                                                                                                       |                                                                                                                                                                                                                    |
| Full document with all internal annotations visible. Internal threads marked clearly. External threads visible. Complete version history. Full approval chain status. | The document body. Only the external threads they are party to. A clean sign-off interface to approve or request changes. No internal annotations. No internal version history. No internal discussion whatsoever. |
+-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------+

This replaces the current workflow of: email PDF to client ‚Üí client marks up Word doc ‚Üí email back ‚Üí manually reconcile changes ‚Üí email again. Chronicle collapses that loop into a single shared view, while keeping firm deliberation entirely private.
